Top Summer Offerings:
 Expand Your Mind  

 July 26 – September 6, 2021  
Mental Health & Resilience for Health-Care Workers

Get the coping skills health-care workers need today. Learn—at your own pace—to manage your mental health with this free, online course for U of T alumni on the frontlines. Presented by professors Steve Joordens and Heather Gordon.

One-on-One with Steini

Join the Dalla Lana School of Public Health and guest host Sabina Vohra-Miller as she interviews Dean Steini Brown. The conversation will cover Ontario’s COVID-19 vaccine rollout and how we prepare for future adverse health events.

Free LinkedIn Learning Courses  

Build up your career skills with up to four months of free U of T alumni access to LinkedIn Learning, starting August 20, 2021. Explore a digital library featuring courses in Business, Technology, Design and Creative. While quantities last.

Hart House — Personal Training

 August 10, 2021 
Trainee Rounds Seminars — AI in Medicine

Join the Temerty Faculty of Medicine for a public presentation from two emerging U of T researchers. Anastasia Razdaibiedina and Michael Balas are at the forefront of exploring new ways to incorporate artificial intelligence (AI) into medicine.

  For Kids and Their Adults  
I Am… A Scientist — Videos  

Learn how an immunologist saves your life or ponder the universe with an astrophysicist. Science Rendezvous U of T aims to bring science to kids of all ages. Their “I Am... A Scientist” video series shows younger audiences what different scientific fields focus on and who scientists actually are.
